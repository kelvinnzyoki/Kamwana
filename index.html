Care Instructions

Clothing:

Wash cold, hang dry. Iron on low heat if needed.

Wood Pieces:

Polish with natural beeswax every 6 months. Keep away from direct sunlight.

Shipping & Returns

How long is delivery?

5-7 business days for fashion items. 14 days for custom wood carvings. Complimentary shipping on orders over $200.

What is your return policy?

30-day returns on unworn clothing with original tags. Wood carvings are final sale due to their custom nature.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International duties and taxes may apply.
